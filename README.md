# powerbi-hr-analytics-dashboard
HR Analytics Dashboard – Power BI

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of employee attrition across multiple dimensions such as age, gender, education, salary, job role, and years at the company. The dashboard is designed to help HR managers and business leaders understand workforce trends, identify high-risk attrition groups, and make data-driven retention decisions.
Key metrics such as total employees, attrition count, attrition rate, average age, average salary, and average tenure are highlighted at the top for quick insights. Interactive visuals allow users to explore attrition patterns by education field, age group, salary slab, job role, department, and years of service.

Key Insights Highlighted in the Dashboard
-Overall attrition rate is 20.63%, indicating a significant workforce challenge.
-Sales Executives and Sales Representatives show the highest attrition.
-Employees with lower salary slabs (up to 10k) experience higher attrition.
-Attrition is most common among employees aged 26–35.
-Higher attrition is observed during the early years at the company.
-Gender-wise attrition shows a higher count among male employees.
